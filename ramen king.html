King ramen

A luxurious noodle dish

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. This dish consists of Chinese wheat noodles served with meat or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and using toppings such as thinly sliced ​​pork tiny), nori (dried seaweed), menma, and scallions. There are many famous ramen brands in the world with a long history such as: Ha chiban ramen,IPPUDO Ramen, Danbo ramen,.. Getting started Since the 1980s, ramen has become a cultural icon of Japan and has been studied worldwide from many angles. At the same time, local versions of ramen were brought to market countries and can even be sorted by their region name. A ramen museum opened in Yokohama in 1994.
